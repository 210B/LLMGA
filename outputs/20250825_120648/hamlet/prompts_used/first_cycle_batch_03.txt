### 1) Core Lens  
**Attend to evidence of:**
- **NPC/LLM behavioral consistency**: identity stability, statement conflicts, speech style (is it period-appropriate? plausible for the role?), and presence of repetitive/templated language.
- **Triggers of immersion and its loss**, including moments when “AI-ness” is perceived (such as self-disclosure, logical breaks, or flat/unresponsive behavior).
- **Visual and design factors**: Visual cues, color, and style supporting or undermining sense of presence/worldbuilding beyond dialogue.
