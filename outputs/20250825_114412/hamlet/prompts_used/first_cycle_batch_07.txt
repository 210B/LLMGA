## 1. Core Lens

Pay particular attention to:
- **LLM/NPC Consistency:** Note dialogue patterns (are responses internally consistent, historically plausible, or do they reveal contradictions, repetition, or 'AI-ness'?).
- **Immersion Signals:** Identify when participants express changes in immersion (몰입/빠져듦), note explicit mentions of 흥미, 관심, or 해당 AI의 인위적 느낌 포착.

## 3. Triggers → Expected Emotions → Immersion Shift Patterns

| Trigger                                                                 | Expected Emotion(s)           | Immersion Shift       |
|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------|
| NPC contradicts itself or changes story details abruptly                | Frustration, Confusion        | immersion↓            |
| NPC issues repetitive responses                                         | Boredom, Frustration          | immersion↓            |
| NPC uses period-appropriate/historically plausible language & behavior  | Interest, Joy, Awe            | immersion↑            |
| NPC acknowledges being AI or responds unnaturally                       | Disappointment, Confusion     | immersion↓            |
| Visual/design elements support story world                              | Joy, Interest, Awe            | immersion↑            |
| NPC adapts well to user’s language/inputs                               | Interest, Curiosity, Joy      | immersion↑            |
| NPC responds with flat, generic, or only minimal information            | Boredom, Disappointment       | immersion↓            |
| Narrative progression feels too abrupt                                  | Confusion, Disappointment     | immersion↓            |
| Player able to rationalize/justify odd NPC behavior (narrative context) | Mixed, Curiosity              | immersion↔/↑           |

---

## 4. Consistency/Design Checklists

- [ ] **dialogue_repetition:** Is the same or similar phrase repeated with little variation?
- [ ] **contradiction:** Does the NPC contradict prior statements or game events?
- [ ] **period_appropriate_speech:** Is the NPC’s language and behavior consistent with the game’s time period?
- [ ] **historical_plausibility:** Are responses and events believable within the historical/story context?
- [ ] **visual_supports_immersion:** Do graphics, color palette, or artistic choices help immersion?
- [ ] **flat_response:** Are answers overly bland, generic, or info-starved?
- [ ] **none:** No noteworthy consistency/design issues.
