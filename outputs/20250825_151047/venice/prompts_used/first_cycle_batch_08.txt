### 1. Core Lens (What to Pay Attention To)
- **a) LLM/NPC Consistency:**

### 3. Triggers → Expected Emotions → Immersion Shift Patterns

| Trigger                                      | Expected Emotion(s)                        | Immersion Shift     |
|-----------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|---------------------|
| NPC/LLM contradicts itself or story           | Confusion, Frustration, Disappointment     | ↓ (lower)           |
| Repetitive or template-like responses         | Boredom, Frustration                       | ↓                   |
| NPC refers to itself as “AI” out-of-context   | Confusion, Boredom, Disappointment         | ↓                   |
| NPC shows period-appropriate/historically plausible speech | Interest, Awe, Joy          | ↑ (higher)           |
| Visual/design elements enhance setting        | Joy, Interest                              | ↑                   |
| Flat, non-descript responses                  | Boredom, Disappointment                    | ↓                   |
| Logical or character-coherent reactions       | Interest, Curiosity, Joy                   | ↑                   |
| Rapid/unexplained emotional shifts from NPC   | Confusion, Disappointment                  | ↓                   |

---

### 4. Consistency/Design Checklists

**Consistency:**
- Is the dialogue contextually consistent? (No contradictions, logical character arc)
- Is the speech appropriate for the time period/setting?
- Does the NPC avoid breaking character (“AI-ness”)?

**Design & Immersion:**
- Do visual/design elements support historical or immersive atmosphere?
- Are responses dynamic vs. repetitive?
- Are emotional and narrative beats fitting the story’s pacing?

**Red Flags for Concern:**
- Dialogue repetition or template-like phrasing
- NPC “outing” as AI unnecessarily
- Flat/unengaged or irrelevant responses
- Missed historical/setting accuracy
